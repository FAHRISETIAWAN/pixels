HOW IT WORKS

  • 1
    TASK SUBMISSION AND DISTRIBUTION
    Clients submit their computational tasks to Asic Miner, where they are instantly distributed across our secure network of high-performance Asic Miner
  • 2
    PROCESSING AND VERIFICATION
    The tasks are processed in parallel, harnessing the power of the L1 Blockchain for secure, efficient, and verifiable computation.
  • 3
    SECURE DELIVERY
    Verified results are encrypted and returned to the clients promptly, ensuring confidentiality and integrity.
